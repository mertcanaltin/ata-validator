# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want ata-validator to be a welcoming project for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and community
- Show empathy towards others

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior can be reported to mertgold60@gmail.com. All reports will be reviewed and addressed.

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this code.
